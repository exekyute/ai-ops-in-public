# Day 005 - Analyze

**Date:** 2026-06-26
**Pillar:** Analyze

## Tidbit

The first question of any metric is not "what is the number," it is "what exactly are we
counting, and what are we dividing it by." Most arguments about whether an automation is
working are really arguments about definitions nobody wrote down. A good metric is one you
could compute by hand from a sample of run logs and get the same answer as the person next to
you. If you cannot do that yet, you do not have a metric, you have a feeling with a decimal
point.
