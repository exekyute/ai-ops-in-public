# Day 002 - Govern

**Date:** 2026-06-23
**Pillar:** Govern

## Tidbit

A standard tells someone how to build. A review checklist tells someone how to check a build
before it goes live, so the quality bar does not live in one person's head. A good review ends
in a decision: approve, approve with fixes, or send back. For anything with a model in it, you
add a few extra questions: is the output checked before something acts on it, is a person
involved when the stakes are real, and is any private data going somewhere it should not.

## Shipped

`governance/ai-build-review-checklist.md` (v1): a short yes/no list a reviewer runs against a
submitted automation, split into basics, AI steps, and documentation, ending in a clear
approve / approve-with-fixes / send-back decision.
