# Day 004 - Document

**Date:** 2026-06-25
**Pillar:** Document

## Tidbit

A document that explains something and a document someone can follow are not the same thing.
A standard or a model tells you what good looks like. An SOP tells a specific person what to
do, in order, with no judgment calls left unexplained. The test is simple: hand it to someone
who has never done the task, and if they get through it without coming to find you, it is a
real SOP. If they have to ask a question halfway through, that question is the part you forgot
to write down.
